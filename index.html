The Honest Conversation

The concerns your IT team won't raise — and the ones they will.

We believe in addressing fears directly. Here are the concerns we hear most, and our honest answers.

"It's too early for AI"

The Fear

AI isn't mature enough. It makes mistakes. We should wait until it's more reliable.

The Reality

16% of UK businesses use AI in any meaningful, identified way (DSIT, 2025) — 80% haven't started. Your competitors aren't waiting. The risk isn't adopting early — it's falling behind permanently.⁴

Our Approach

We start small and measurable. Quick wins that prove value. If it doesn't work, you stop. Minimal risk, maximum learning.

"AI is too risky for our data"

The Fear

Our data is sensitive. We can't send it to the cloud or trust AI with confidential information.

The Reality

Valid concern. 53% of organisations cite data privacy as their top AI blocker.⁵ But that's a reason to own your AI, not avoid it.

Our Approach

We run AI on your infrastructure. Your data never leaves your network. No vendor peeking, no cloud dependency. Your data, your rules.

"We tried ChatGPT and it wasn't useful"

The Fear

We experimented with ChatGPT but it didn't do much for our business. AI is overhyped.

The Reality

Using ChatGPT and deciding AI isn't useful is like using a hammer once and deciding construction doesn't work. A tool isn't a strategy.

Our Approach

We build systems, not chat sessions. Multiple AI capabilities working together, configured for your business, running daily without prompting.
